Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the app. We never sell your data. Your wardrobe photos are yours — we only use them to generate your packing list.

1. Who We Are

Pakme ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered travel packing app. We are the data controller for the personal information collected through our app and website at packme.dev.

Contact us at: hello@packme.dev

2. What We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Pakme:

  • Account information: Your email address and name when you create an account
  • Wardrobe photos: Photos you upload of your clothing items
  • Wardrobe data: AI-generated tags for each item (type, color, formality, occasions)
  • Trip data: Destinations, dates, activities, and AI-generated packing lists you create
  • Style preferences: Your answers to the style quiz
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (screens visited, features used)
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe — we never see your card details

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized packing lists and outfit suggestions using your wardrobe
  • Provide and improve the Pakme service
  • Send you trip reminders and important account notifications
  • Process subscription payments
  • Respond to customer support requests

4. AI Processing

When you upload wardrobe photos, we send them to Anthropic's Claude AI for analysis. This analysis identifies the clothing item type, color, formality, and appropriate occasions. We store the results (tags) in your profile — not the raw analysis request.

Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing: anthropic.com/privacy

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a GDPR-compliant cloud database provider. Wardrobe photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage. All data is stored in the United States.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic: To power AI wardrobe analysis and packing list generation
  • Stripe: To process subscription payments
  • Supabase: To store and manage your account data
  • Law enforcement: If required by law

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@packme.dev

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. This permanently deletes your account, wardrobe photos, trip history, and all associated data within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Pakme is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at hello@packme.dev.

10. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app. Continued use of Pakme after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at hello@packme.dev or write to us at Pakme, New York, NY.
